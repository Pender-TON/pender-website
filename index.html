How Presale works?
Presale - it's the initial sale of tokens or coins to a select group of investors before the public offering.
Min. Invest per Account: 10 TON
Max. Invest per Account: 100 TON
The presale will last for 7 days.
P.S. It's allowed to have more than 1 account.
How to join Presale?
Buy TON on an accessible CEX.
Transfer TON to your wallet (Tonkeeper & TonSpace).
Send TON to the presale wallet.
Wait for your coins to appear in your wallet.
Win 10-100x from your investment.
Remember that the maximum purchase per account is 100 TON.
How Lottery works?
Participation in the lottery is open to every account that sends TON from their wallet to the presale wallet.
There will be at least 1 winner in the lottery. The winner will receive 10% of the total TON collected (taking into account the minimum collected amount of TON - 10,000) - this will be 1000 TON.
The second winner will be chosen after the end of the PRESALE.
